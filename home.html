Musician, Producer, and Film Composer based in Chicago, Illinois.

Isaiah Jones Jr., a versatile artist and composer from Milwaukee, Wisconsin, now based in Chicago. With a decade of musical experience, he discovered his love for piano in church at age eight. Influenced by jazz in his teens, he excelled as a pianist in the Kevin Hayden trio at 17. His education at Northern Illinois University exposed him to diverse musical traditions, shaping his compositions with cultural nuances. Isaiah's background in jazz and gospel makes him a sought-after musician in the Midwest. He expanded into film scoring in his late teens, blending global musical influences into soulful compositions. Isaiah's dedication to translating creative expressions through music reflects his profound passion for his craft.
